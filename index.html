Our Story

A Taste of
Maa Ki Rasoi

Manisha Ka Dhaba was born from one woman's passion — Manisha Ji, who started cooking for travellers on the Jaipur highway in 1998 with just a clay stove, a handful of spices, and generations of family recipes passed down from the Marwar region.

Today, we remain true to those roots. Every dish is cooked on slow fire, with fresh ingredients sourced locally, and served with the same warmth that made Manisha Ji's original dhaba legendary. Come hungry, leave with a full heart.
